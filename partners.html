The business case

Why it is worth it for your practice.

MARKABLE is the objective mapping layer under your midlife care. You treat. We map and track. Here is what that does for the practice.

It saves clinician time, the expensive kind.

The 7-minute mapping is done before the visit, so the appointment opens straight on the care plan instead of history-taking. Across a full schedule, those saved minutes become real clinician capacity, the most expensive resource you have.

It sharpens the patient-clinician conversation.

She arrives with an objective profile and her top symptoms already flagged. Your clinician sees where to focus, and the trend since last visit gives every follow-up a shared, concrete reference point instead of starting from memory.

It keeps a long-term, high-value patient.

Midlife care is a multi-year relationship, not a one-time episode. When she can see her plan working between visits, she stays engaged in her care, and she stays with your practice.
