Why it fits the way you already work

One layer. At scale, or in a single practice.

MARKABLE sits underneath your care model. It does not change how you treat. It changes how much you know when you do.

For virtual care at scale

It slots into short visits, and into your data strategy.

  • The intake is done before the call. The map is complete before she joins, so a 15-minute follow-up can open straight on the care plan.
  • The trend is visible between visits. Each re-map shows which way her signals are moving, so a follow-up starts from what changed.
  • The dataset stays yours. An objective, de-identified longitudinal record that compounds with every visit and sits alongside the wearable data you already use.
  • It is the accessible front door. A simple, early signal that helps flag who to route toward the bone, cardiac and metabolic workups your clinicians already run.
For a clinic or practice

It slots into the appointment you already run.

  • She arrives mapped. The intake is already done, so the appointment opens on care and your time goes to judgment, titration and the plan.
  • It carries your brand. The whole experience is white-labeled in your name, with MARKABLE underneath.
  • She can see her own trend. Between visits she watches her signals move, a reason to keep her follow-ups.
  • You offer it your way. Include it in your program, or as a premium tier.
